About ICTips

Content:

- Programming tips for the incoming IT students.
- Open forum for the past experiences of the IT students.
- Lessons that is going to be offered for the incoming IT students.
- Must and must not for the incoming IT students in terms of programming.
- Registration and login form.
- Programming languages tips.
- Programming practices and activities.

Purpose:

- Guide for the incoming IT students.

Explanation:
Me personally as i began my journey in ICT strand way back Grade 11, i find it hard to cope with other students in IT strand because i didn't find any guides related to my strand. So i thought of this to help the future IT students and make IT easier for them.

- Enhance a student's confident for his/her strand.

Explanation:
For me, if a student is confident about his or her strand they will most likely be able to understand the lesson smoothly and are most likely active in class.

- Provide extra knowledge for incoming IT students.

Explanation:
More knowledge is equals to a better academic performance for the students.

- By this, the teacher doesn't have to discuss thoroughly.

Explanation:
If a students have extra knowledge about a particular topic, the teacher can focus on enhancing a student's programming skills rather than discussin firmly a certain topic.

- Enhances a student's skills in programming.

Explanation:
Skills in reality and skills in programming is different, thats why i came up with an idea which will ease the life of the future IT students by helping them enhancing their skills. This could be done by giving such programming activities and practices.

- Enhances a student's way of socializing to his/her classmates.

Explanation:
I personally added a registration and login section so that the students could also share their experiences, suggestions and socialize to other students as well.
